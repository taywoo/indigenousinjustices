    Justice for
    Joyce Echaquan

    A young Atikamekw woman died while facing incredibly racist and insensitive remarks by Quebec public health care staff.

    What Happened?

    It is the latest in a series of incidents that has once again exhibited the severe levels of systemic racism that Indigenous citizens must face in Canada.

    Ms Echaquan, a 37-year-old Atikamekw woman and mother of 7, had gone to the Joliette hospital about 70km from Montreal suffering from stomach pains. She filmed from her hospital bed screaming and calling for help. The staff can be heard saying to her, in French: “You’re stupid as hell.” Another says Ms Echaquan had made bad choices in life and asks what her children would think of her behaviour.

    Ms Echaquan died soon afterwards. Her relatives told Radio-Canada that she had a history of heart trouble and was worried that she was being given too much morphine.
