    • Indigenous Injustices is a visual guide examining ongoing attacks on Indigenous people in Canada.

    The four events featured here are merely a drop in the ocean of racism, discrimination and injustice that Indigenous people have faced for hundreds of years here in Canada. They are not just dealing with the generational effects of colonization. As this project documents, it is an ongoing affair and it must come to an end.

    This project was created by Taylor Reddam-Woo for DART449 at Concordia University which resides on unceded Indigenous lands. The Kanien’kehá:ka Nation is recognized as the custodians of the lands and waters on which we gather today.
